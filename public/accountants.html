Verification

Professional body
Enter your ACCA, ICAEW, CPA, ICATT, ICAJ or other membership number. We check against their registry automatically. Instant for most bodies.

FAQ

Common questions

Is it really free to list my practice? ↓
Yes — completely free. There is no monthly fee, no setup fee, and no charge for being listed in the directory. FinFlow only makes money when you make money — through the 4% service commission on payments processed through the platform.
How does the 4% commission work? ↓
When you bill a client through FinFlow, they pay via Stripe. FinFlow automatically takes 4% of the payment and transfers 96% directly to your connected bank account via Stripe Connect. You never have to chase payments or do manual splits.
Do I have to use FinFlow to bill clients? ↓
You're not technically forced to, but billing through FinFlow is how you build verified reviews, protect your listing, and maintain your referral commission income. Off-platform payments violate our Terms of Service and can result in suspension from the directory.
When do I get paid my referral commissions? ↓
Referral commissions are paid on the 1st of every month for all active, paying clients in your portfolio. If a client cancels their subscription, their commission stops immediately. When they resubscribe, it resumes for the remaining months.
What countries are supported? ↓
FinFlow supports accountants from any country. Payments are processed in USD via Stripe, which supports payouts to most countries globally. We specifically support professional bodies in Trinidad & Tobago, Jamaica, the UK, US, Canada, and Ireland.
How long does verification take? ↓
Professional body verification is typically instant if your membership number is in their registry. Employer and institution verification is completed by our team within 1 business day. You'll receive an email confirmation when your profile goes live.
