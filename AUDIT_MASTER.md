## F32 decision note (recognition basis тАФ DECIDED 18 Jul 2026)

**Basis: ACCRUAL, ISSUE-BASED.** Revenue is recognized when an invoice is **issued** (Dr AR / Cr Revenue), not when paid. Settlements (`invoice_payments`, and legacy `payments_received`) draw down AR and are **never** revenue (Dr Cash / Cr AR). `sales_receipts` are cash sales with no invoice (Dr Cash / Cr Revenue). This makes the Outstanding/AR card meaningful. **Cash basis** (receipts + collections only) becomes a **future toggle**, not the default. Note: this is a deliberate shift from the app's current *paid-invoice* recognition тАФ under issue-based accrual, an unpaid issued invoice **is** revenue **and** AR.

**Payments route to STORE B** (`invoice_payments`, keyed to a real `invoice_id`, flips status via `recalcInvoiceStatus`, supports partials). The three `openRecordPaymentModal` definitions collapse to one that passes the invoice id/client/amount through (see **F35**).

**Store A (`payments_received`) disposition тАФ options (recommend #1):**
1. **Deprecate + migrate (recommended).** One-time migration of existing rows: if a row's `invoice_ref` matches an invoice тЖТ recreate it as an `invoice_payments` (Store B) settlement against that invoice (then `recalcInvoiceStatus`); if it matches nothing тЖТ treat as a cash sale тЖТ recreate as a `sales_receipts` row. 2. **Keep, reclassified as settlement-only.** Retain the table but remove it from revenue and never let it be a recognition leg тАФ it becomes a manual AR-settlement log parallel to Store B. Weaker: duplicates Store B's job without flipping status, so AR/Outstanding still won't move.
3. **Minimal: exclude from revenue, leave as-is.** Just drop the `payments_received` leg from the formula and leave the page. Smallest change, but leaves a confusing dead-end store that doesn't affect any figure. *Not recommended.*

Migration must be reversible/dry-runnable and gated on my approval тАФ it rewrites historical money rows.
